## 원문

At ATLAB® Middle East, one of our core missions is to support universities in achieving their research goals, through advanced educational products, hands-on training programs, and fully integrated lab setups.
As part of this vision, I recently delivered a specialized training at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals on Qarm Quanser system, enabling deeper exploration into robotics, AI, and intelligent systems.
The training was highly practical and focused on:
🔹 Deep dive into the QArm system and its key hardware components
🔹 Real-time control applications using MATLAB & QUARC
🔹 Advanced Computer Vision using the Intel camera
🔹 Hands- project implementation, including:
• Object Detection
• Object Servoing
We also explored advanced topics such as:
• AI integration in robotics
• IoT and smart systems vision
The entire experience was built around hands on learning from system setup to executing real world projects combining AI + Robotics + Vision Systems.
Thank for all attendees, special thanks for Dr. Brahim Brahmi Ing. Msc. PhD for coordinating the session, and for Ali Mustafa for his support and engagment.
This marks another step in our journey of empowering universities and researchers to build impactful, real world research solutions.
