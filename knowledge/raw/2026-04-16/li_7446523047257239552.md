## 원문

🤔 Your AI-assisted developers are merging more code than ever.
Yet - I gonna have to just say it... -
That does not mean your organisation is automatically shipping more [quality] value.
👉 Faros AI tracked 10,000+ developers across 1,255 teams.
High AI adoption meant 21% more tasks completed and 98% more PRs merged.
But review time went up 91%. PR size went up 154%.
And delivery improvement at org level was rather limited.
https://lnkd.in/eaSXer2J
Then
👉 LinearB showed AI-generated PRs waiting 4.6x longer for review, with much lower acceptance rates than manual code. https://lnkd.in/eT-fnyr6
So no, I do not think this [human] PR review Stat Tracking is the hill to die on.
I think we are measuring the wrong thing.
- PR count is not value.
- Lines changed are not value.
- Review time is not value.
The question is what reached production, solved something real, and did so safely.
AI is scaling code creation faster than humans can manually inspect it.
- It is better quality gates and controls
- Better policies and guard-rails
- Better release design (remember canary, blue-green?)
- And humans focused on accountability, not pretending they can manually review infinite output.
