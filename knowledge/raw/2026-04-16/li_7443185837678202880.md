97% of AI organizations now depend on real-time web data.
That alone isn’t surprising.
What I find more interesting is that 90% say they feel constrained by it.
That stat comes from Bright Data’s latest report, AI for Data Infrastructure 2026.
As AI systems become more capable, speed, reliability, and compliance at the data layer are starting to matter a lot more.
You can see the shift in how organizations are responding.
It’s no longer just about improving the model itself.
More attention is going into the infrastructure around it - making sure AI can access fresh information, use it in real time, and do that in a reliable, compliant way.
Even now, one old rule still applies: garbage in, garbage out.
The edge is moving toward better systems, not just better models.
