## 원문

In 2022, less than 5% of knowledge workers used AI tools regularly.
By 2024, that number crossed 60%.
That's not adoption. That's a stampede.
But here's what the numbers don't show most people learned how to use the tool. Almost nobody learned how to think differently because of it.
There's a difference between a person who uses GPS and a person who understands navigation. One is dependent. The other is capable.
We're building a generation of GPS users.
Fast? Yes. Efficient? Absolutely. But what happens when the signal drops?
AI is not just a productivity upgrade. It's a cognitive shift. And cognitive shifts don't happen just because you downloaded an app or paid for a subscription.
They happen when you sit with discomfort long enough to rewire how you approach problems.
That part? Nobody's selling a course on it. Because it doesn't have a clean before/after. It doesn't go viral.
The hype cycle moves at the speed of a tweet.
Human adaptation moves at the speed of lived experience.
And right now, we're confusing one for the other.
