## 원문

If you want to understand AI agents in practice, start with voice agents.
What makes them useful is that the full agent loop is visible in one place:
---- input, transcription, reasoning, response, and action, all running in real time.
That matters because once the loop is fast and stable enough, the system stops being just a voice interface.
It becomes a working layer for things like appointment booking, support routing, CRM updates, follow-ups, and other repetitive call workflows.
That’s why voice agents are becoming one of the more practical forms of agentic AI.
This visual shows that architecture quite clearly.
And ElevenLabs is one of the companies building in this category, with voice agents that connect to business systems and handle tasks like scheduling, routing, and CRM-linked interactions.
