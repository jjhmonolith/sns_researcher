## 원문

Breaking: Google launch Mac app for Gemini. And it changes how Gemini sits inside your workflow.
Click "Option + Space". That's it.
No new tab. No copy-paste shuffle. No breaking your train of thought to go "ask the AI."
A few things stand out for anyone working in or around schools:
- Window sharing is the real headline. You can show Gemini anythign you are working on and ask about that specific thing. No retyping. No describing what you're looking at. The AI sees what you see.
- The "stay in flow" promise is an important one. Drafting an email and need to soften a paragraph? Reviewing a report and need a quick summary? Building a scheme of work and need a starter activity? You no longer leave the document to get help with the document.
- Image and video generation built in. Nano Banana for images, Veo for video, directly from your desktop.
Gemini has moved from destination to layer.
It's free and available now globally.
