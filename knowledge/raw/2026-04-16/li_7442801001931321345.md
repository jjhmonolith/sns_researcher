17,000 companies just revealed what AI skills actually matter in 2026.
The scale on the AI side is bigger than a lot of people probably realize:
- Microsoft Copilot learning for business: +3,400% YoY
- AI agents and agentic AI: +13,534% YoY
But alongside that, more judgment-based skills are rising too:
- Critical thinking: +37% YoY
- Decision-making: +38% YoY
To me, that’s the more interesting part of the story:
Companies are not just racing to build AI capability, they’re also investing in the ability to use AI well.
As more repeatable work gets automated, the edge may increasingly go to people who can interpret, decide, and apply AI in context.
