## 원문

Samora AI (YC W26) just came out of YC building multilingual voice agents. They call themselves "Figma for voice agents."
Multilingual is no longer a nice-to-have. It's a funded category.
But here's what breaks when you go multilingual: ASR accuracy drops 15-40% depending on the language. Code-switching between languages mid-sentence confuses most STT models. And latency spikes because translation adds another round trip.
We benchmarked 49 languages for word error rate across the major STT providers. The gaps are massive.
If you're building multilingual voice agents, test them in every language you ship. Not just English with a Spanish demo.
