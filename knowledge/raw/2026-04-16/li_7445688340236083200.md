## 원문

If AI is getting stronger, and enterprises are doing more around it too - better infrastructure, more workflow design, more governance, more attention to information quality -
why does large-scale adoption still feel so hard?
Is the harder part in tacit knowledge, workflow logic, exceptions, relationship context, and the judgment people carry in their heads?
That’s the thread I explore in this week’s issue -
and why the real work may now be less about adding more AI, and more about building the knowledge architecture and workflow environment around it.
