## 원문

Most engineering leaders can tell you their Copilot adoption rate.
Almost none can tell you if it moved their cycle time.
That gap is the problem I keep running into.
So here's a framework I've been using with engineering leaders across India and APAC — four things worth actually measuring in the AI era:
R — ROI Visibility, Where is engineering investment going? Not story points. Real effort, by category — features, QA, maintenance — in hours and cost. That's what HQ asks for. Most teams can't answer it.
I — Intelligence, DORA metrics tell you aggregate health. They don't show you that 40% of every PR's lifetime is sitting in review queue. Stage-by-stage cycle time does. That's where you find the actual bottleneck.
S — Speed Integrity, AI made your team code faster. Did it make them ship faster? Those are different questions. Compare AI-assisted PR cycle time to your baseline — full cycle, not just coding phase. Also track code churn within 21 days. Fast generation without that check is just technical debt on a timer.
E — Engineering Experience, If satisfaction drops while delivery metrics improve, you're borrowing against your team. Survey data catches that before exit interviews do.
We call it the RISE Framework. Built it specifically for GCC engineering leaders who need to go into HQ reviews with something better than an adoption dashboard.
