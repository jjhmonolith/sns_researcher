## 원문

From the first time I walked into a school in June 2023, I've been trying to understand what teachers and students need to know to use GenAI in safe, ethical, and effective ways.
Through a lot of trial and error and direct feedback from educators, we developed the SEE Framework, the first AI literacy framework specifically designed for GenAI.
Today we're launching the full version: A Practical Guide to Building Generative AI Literacy.
What we found is that when training is grounded in knowledge of how GenAI actually works and the mindsets that shape how people engage with it, educators and students are able to approach the tools in intentional and critical ways. That's the foundation the framework is built on.
For the full launch, we also created:
✅ Age-appropriate guidance from Pre-K through adult, including a clear stance on tool use at each stage, because permissibility isn't the same as appropriateness
✅ Framework in Action examples showing what Safe + Ethical + Effective looks like in real scenarios: a middle schooler using an AI tutor, a high schooler writing a college essay, a teacher deciding whether to use AI detection tools
✅ GenAI literacy activity banks for young learners, secondary students, and adults
✅ A commitment to keep updating the Framework as the research and the technology evolve
This was a team effort with contributions across the entire AI for Education team.
You can download the full framework in the links below. Let us know what you think!
And if you'd like to learn more, we are doing a walk-through of the framework at 12 EST today (you can sign up to join or get the recording in the link in the comments).
