## 원문

After an incredibly rewarding 19-year chapter, I am starting a new journey beyond my role at Affinity Equity Partners.
Over this time, I’ve had the privilege of working on some of the most meaningful private equity investments in Korea—but for me, it was never just about the transaction.
The question I kept coming back to was simple:
How do we fundamentally build a better company?
A few outcomes that reflect this approach:
Value Creation
* Oriental Brewery – from USD 1.8Bn -> USD 5.8Bn
* ServeOne – led a full stack operational transformation, embedding platform and solution capabilities on a global scale (est. EV of ~3Tn) (https://lnkd.in/gCRGSN-S)
* JobKorea – repositioned through a full AX journey into an AI-based HR Tech platform
* Ubase – full AX transformation into a leading AICC operator
* Burger King Japan – 5.8x MoM, exiting at ~20x EBITDA multiple (https://lnkd.in/gjZ88ZDu)
* Burger King Korea – building a leading multi-brand digital QSR platform
* SK Rent A Car – Carve-out to full transformation
Tough Rescues
* Kyobo – one of the most complex situations, following years of legal challenges; successfully exited in ~1.5 years
* SSG – navigating a difficult sector exit above cost
* LocknLock – successful turnaround to substantial recovery of value in ~2 years
* Oriental Brewery – securing two of the largest tax refunds in Korea PE history
Building Scalable Capabilities
* Value creation playbook
* Ops Group strategy
* AI transformation strategy
None of this would have been possible without a group of exceptional individuals I had the privilege to work closely with in Korea—people who consistently pushed boundaries, challenged convention, and built in ways not previously attempted. I’m deeply grateful for the trust, the intensity, and the shared ambition.
What I am most proud of is the tangible value we created—driving operational improvements, enabling BX and AX transformation, and building stronger, aspirational, world-class companies.
Through these experiences, one belief has only strengthened:
true value creation goes far beyond financial engineering.
It comes from fundamentally improving how businesses operate—through disciplined execution, technology, and better decision-making.
This is also what I’ve found most energizing—working alongside teams to rethink how companies operate and seeing that translate into real, measurable outcomes.
And increasingly, that means embedding AI not as a tool, but as a core part of how businesses run.
As I look ahead, I am focused on building and partnering with companies to drive meaningful transformation at scale—combining AI, operational excellence, and repeatable value creation frameworks.
On to the next chapter.
