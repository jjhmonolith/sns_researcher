## 원문

Had an interesting conversation last week.
A company brought us in to check what AI tools their employees were actually using. Not the ones IT approved. The ones people found on their own.
They thought maybe 2 or 3 tools had slipped through.
We found 11.
Client contracts getting pasted into free AI summarizers. HR documents running through translation tools nobody vetted. Finance teams using AI to clean up reports before board meetings.
None of it malicious. All of it well-intentioned. Every single person was just trying to work faster.
But the data was already gone.
The thing is, this isn't a policy problem. Most of these companies already have AI policies. The problem is they have zero visibility into what's actually happening day to day.
You can't govern what you can't see.
We now do this as a standalone assessment. 48 hours. No software installed on anyone's laptop. No employee monitoring. Just a clear picture of where your data is actually going.
Every company we've shown the results to has been surprised. Not one has said "yeah, that's what we expected.
