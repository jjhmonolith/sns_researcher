## 원문

Seriously, if you generate something with AI and then share it with your teammates, at least read it first.
Dumping long, unfiltered AI output into a chat or document without even reading it yourself is just passing the burden to someone else.
The common problems are pretty obvious:
* The topic wasn’t even relevant to the original discussion
* The content is often partially wrong or hallucinated
* It’s unnecessarily long and painful to read
* And worst of all, the person who shared it clearly didn’t read it
People ask AI questions without understanding the context, then blindly paste the result as if it adds value but no.
If you wouldn’t read it yourself, don’t send it to others!!!!!!
If it’s hard for you to read, it’s hard for everyone else too 😡
And one more thing
If you’re adding AI generated text into an existing document, at least match the formatting and tone.
When a well-structured document suddenly has a chunk of mismatched style and voice in the middle, obviously you didn’t read it, you just pasted it.
Use AI however you want. But if you’re sharing the output with others, take responsibility for it. Otherwise, you’re not helping.
