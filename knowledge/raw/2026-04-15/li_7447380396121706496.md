It's been 3 months since I joined Solidroad as an AI software engineer, and I couldn't be more excited about the road ahead!
Solidroad is the quality platform for support teams, helping companies QA and train their human and AI agents across thousands of daily conversations. What drew me in was the mission: using AI not to replace the human side of support, but to preserve it. Warmth, empathy, intuition. That's worth building for.
After 3 years building Lambda's core systems at Amazon Web Services (AWS), the jump to an early-stage startup has been the best kind of challenge. The past 3 months have been a blast. Moving fast, shipping constantly, and learning something new every day with such a talent-dense team. Huge thanks to Mark Hughes and Patrick Finlay for their relentless energy and leadership.
Solidroad is backed by Y Combinator and First Round Capital, and we're hiring! If you want to work on hard AI problems with an in-person team in San Francisco: check out solidroad.com/careers
