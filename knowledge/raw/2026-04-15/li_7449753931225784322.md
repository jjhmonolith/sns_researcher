⚡️ Beyond Solver. Toward Physics-Aware AI.
AI can now understand language, generate images, and write code but when it comes to physics, it is still limited. Gravity, heat transfer, material deformation, fluid flow: we believe AI still has a long way to go before it can truly understand and reason about the laws that govern the physical world.
At SolverX, we’re developing Physics-Aware AI that tightly combines the physical knowledge in numerical simulation with real data from manufacturing environments. We accelerate simulations from hours to seconds and create learning loops where field data continually deepens the AI’s physical understanding. This is why SolverX is always working together with manufacturers that have spent decades pushing the limits of materials, processes, and design.
We’re excited to share this story at Berkeley Innovation Forum (BIF) 2026. This year’s theme, “Designing Growth Through New, More Open Business Models”, is closely aligned with the changes we are seeing in the field, and we’re looking forward to having this conversation with global leaders in energy, materials, and manufacturing.
