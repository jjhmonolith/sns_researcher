## 원문

If AI keeps absorbing more junior tasks, how should companies redesign the talent pipeline?
Junior roles were never just about output.
A lot of what later becomes judgment, context, and senior capability used to get built there.
And this does not stop at juniors.
AI is also starting to reshape what senior value actually means.
For most of us, the answer is probably not just “learn AI.”
It is to understand what layer of value becomes more important once output gets cheaper.
That’s what today’s issue is about.
