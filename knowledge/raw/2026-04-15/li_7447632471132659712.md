## 원문

My friend runs an e-commerce business. Around 250 orders a day.
Every morning she opens Shopify on her phone, checks each address manually, copy pastes the same WhatsApp message to customers with incomplete addresses, and batches orders in groups of 50 for her courier.
This weekend I built an automation that handles most of that. It pulls unfulfilled orders from Shopify, checks every address using AI, flags customers who previously refused deliveries, sends WhatsApp messages automatically, and groups clean orders into batches of 50.
Most of those boxes replace something she was doing by hand every morning.
Still plugging in the final credentials. But the logic is built.
The interesting part wasn't the automation. It was realizing I knew nothing about her actual process until I asked. Spent an hour just mapping how she works before writing a single node.
Same thing I do before any consulting engagement, just with different tools.
