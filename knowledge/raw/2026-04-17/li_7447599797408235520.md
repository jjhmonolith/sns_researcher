## 원문

Rather than simply deploying LLM agents in educational settings, it is necessary to design and implement pedagogically purposeful conversations aligned with explicit instructional goals. Although we have previously published a study proposing an LMS with such capabilities, translating that concept into a fully operational system remains challenging in practice. The study frames this kind of AI-enabled LMS in terms of configurable prompts, adaptive feedback loops, and multi-agent conversation flows grounded in pedagogical principles.
https://lnkd.in/g-gnutPm
Therefore, I am currently preparing a proof of concept by constructing an LLM orchestration that interacts with learners according to dialogue scenarios designed for specific educational purposes. In practical terms, the orchestration integrates learner data, builds a learner-profile twin, and guides interaction through tailored conversational paths rather than relying on a single general-purpose agent.
