## 원문

Most people think AI grading replaces the instructor.
We built Pensive to do the opposite: to amplify them.
I joined Alex Sarlin on the Edtech Insiders podcast to talk about what we've learned building Pensive across 100+ universities.
My favorite example: University of California, Berkeley's one of the largest data science courses used Pensive to free up so much TA grading time that they launched small-group tutoring sessions for the first time. TAs went from grading papers to actually teaching.
That's the unlock - switching screen time to student time.
Thanks to Alex Sarlin, Ben Kornell, and the Edtech Insiders team for having me on. Full interview link in the comments 👇
