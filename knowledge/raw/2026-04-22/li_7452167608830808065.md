## 원문

I thought automation 🤖 would make accountants obsolete.
Reality? It’s finally making them valuable.
In one mid-sized company, the accounts team spent 6+ hours daily on just three things:
📌 Chasing invoices
📌 Reconciling entries
📌 Managing GST filings
None of it created insight ❌
None of it improved decisions ❌
All of it drained capable talent ⚠️
Then they introduced simple automation layers over existing systems:
⚙️ OCR + RPA for invoice capture and posting
⚙️ Automated bank and ledger reconciliations
⚙️ Rule-based workflows for GST and tax compliance
Within 90 days ⏱️:
📉 Manual effort reduced by ~60%
🚀 Faster, cleaner month-end closures
💡 Teams shifted focus to cash flow, forecasting, and decision support
The real shift wasn’t technology. It was mindset 🧠
The question changed from:
❓ “Will automation replace us?”
To:
💭 “What can we do now that we finally have time to think?”
Here’s how the future of finance looks to me:
1️⃣ 80–90% of transaction work will be automated 🤖
2️⃣ Insight, judgment, and communication will define value 💬
3️⃣ Those who learn to manage automation will lead the next decade 🏆
I’m working with teams transitioning from spreadsheet-driven operations 📊 to insight-led finance functions 📈
If you’re exploring this shift, comment “AUTOMATION” ⬇️ or message me 📩 — I can share a simple 3-step roadmap that works
