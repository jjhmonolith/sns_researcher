## 원문

If AI displaces workers faster than the economy can reabsorb them, it destroys the very consumer demand that firms depend on to grow.
The uncomfortable part? Knowing this does not stop it.
Competitive pressure drives firms into a Nash Equilibrium where automation runs well beyond what is collectively rational. Task-based displacement models show that agentic systems absorb high-decomposability work first: boilerplate, test scaffolding, API integration, bug triaging.
What remains is residual complexity that cannot be automated but also cannot be handled by a team that has been gutted.
The firms that win will be the ones whose developers can define agent objective functions, validate AI outputs against architectural constraints, and keep agentic behavior aligned with real system requirements.
That skill set does not emerge on its own. It has to be built deliberately, on your own codebases, with your own teams.
