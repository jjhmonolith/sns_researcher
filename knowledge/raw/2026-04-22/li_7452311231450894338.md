## 원문

A full-scale commercial, crafted with AI — no production, just direction.
I recently created a cinematic AI ad for Bla Bli Blu Oud Honey, built entirely from a single room in just one week.
This project explores how AI can be used not just as a generative tool, but as a directed medium for filmmaking and advertising.
No set. No crew. No traditional shoot.
Every frame, cut, and mood was designed with intent.
As part of the process, I also pushed the boundaries of AI generation by experimenting with double exposure aesthetics—creating layered, symbolic imagery beyond standard outputs.
For me, this wasn’t about speed or cost.
It was about control, authorship, and cinematic language within AI workflows.
🎬 Key focus areas:
• AI-driven commercial filmmaking
• Visual storytelling using generative tools
• Experimentation with advanced visual styles (double exposure)
• End-to-end direction using AI pipelines
🧠 Tools used:
Higgsfield AI · Seedance 2.0 · Kling 3.0 · Nano Banana 2 · DaVinci Resolve · Logic Pro · Suno AI
💬 I’d be interested to hear how others are approaching AI in advertising and filmmaking.
