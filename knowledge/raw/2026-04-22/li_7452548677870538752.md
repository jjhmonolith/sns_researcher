## 원문

It is a tough market out there. Each hyperscaler HAS to provide every model out there (because everyone wants to be model agnostic) and yet they make more money when the corporation sticks to the model the hyperscaler provides.
Had this experience firsthand recently with Google reps trying excessively hard to sell Google Gemini Enterprise (sticking to Google's models) over Google's Vertex AI (a model agnostic platform) to build custom Agentic workflows.
A little something that makes it harder for corporations to adopt Agentic AI.
