## 원문

An HR team of 3 just processed 800 job applications in 10 minutes. No, that's not a typo.
Here's exactly what that looks like — and why it changes everything you thought you knew about hiring.
Before AI:
Post a role → wait 5 days → manually screen 800 CVs → schedule 20 interviews → pray you picked the right shortlist.
With AI:
Post goes live → AI screens all 800 applications in minutes → surfaces the top 10 with reasoning → hiring manager reviews in 30 minutes → interviews scheduled automatically.
Same job. Same candidates. A fraction of the time.
Unilever, L'Oréal, and Telstra are already running this process. So are hundreds of companies you've never heard of.
The result?
• Hiring cycles cut from 6 weeks to 8 days
• Bias reduced at the screening stage
• Candidates get faster responses
• HR team focuses on humans, not admin
AI doesn't take the job. It removes the part nobody wanted anyway.
What would your HR team do with that time back?
