## 원문

Finished an on-site AI tools workshop for a large APAC MNC in Singapore.
Explored AI chatbots for workflow improvements focusing on Copilot (enterprise grade security needed), Claude (it's the in thing recently).
The highlight was definitely the video workflow (the most oohs and ahhs heard):
-Planning (human in the loop)
-Story Expansion
-AI Image + Video Prompt Creation
-3 levels of AI Video Creation (sound and music might be separate but we didn't do it)
-Stitching in a video editor
We finished with how agentic AI can help MNCs with productivity boosts, without all the clickbait nonsense about replacing entire teams in 2026 (again, human in the loop was something very important in a trust focused industry)
Glad to have brought some value to an in-house team on improving productivity from usual document/ pptn creation to prototyping with AI image and video creation for media agency alignment (I did mention that current workflows don't necessarily replace creative agencies for now).
I continue to offer this customised workshop to companies looking for similar solutions.
