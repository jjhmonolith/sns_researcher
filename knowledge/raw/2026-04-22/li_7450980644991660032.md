## 원문

We’ve collected data from over 1,000 students who have completed our new GenAI Literacy 101 for Students course since we launched in February, and what we’re seeing makes a compelling case for why structured AI literacy instruction matters right now.
COURSE IMPACT DATA (based on student's self-ratings after taking the course):
📈 61% completion rate (4-5x the average for MOOCs)
📈 77% will be more ethical users of GenAI
📈 66% will be more careful about when and how they use GenAI
📈 65% (up 28% from the pre-survey) now rate themselves highly in their understanding of GenAI's capabilities
📈 56% gained confidence in deciding when it's okay to use GenAI for school
📈 55% will think more critically about GenAI outputs
We are excited to see that even 2 hours of intentional GenAI literacy built on our SEE framework can lead to meaningful shifts for students.
Some of the key features of the course:
✔️ Free for high school and college students to take on their own
✔️ Hands-on activities and demos of safe, ethical, effective use of GenAI tools
✔️ Practical frameworks students can apply immediately
✔️ Interactive checks to help students stay focused and retain what they're learning
✔️ Videos with human-verified captions, transcripts, and audio descriptions (WCAG compliant)
