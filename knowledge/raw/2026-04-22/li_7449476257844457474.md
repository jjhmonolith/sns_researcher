After seeing countless AI initiatives stall, one pattern is clear:
It’s rarely the model that fails.
It’s everything around it.
From unclear business KPIs to messy real-world data, missing production architecture, ignored governance, and lack of ownership most POCs were never designed to survive beyond the demo.
The real shift isn’t technical.
It’s operational.
If you're working on AI (or planning to), this might change how you think about success:
➼ It’s no longer “Can we build it?”
➼ It’s “Can we operationalize it?”
