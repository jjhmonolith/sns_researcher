🚀 Finance Transformation: From Transactions to Insights (in 24 Weeks)
Most finance teams don’t lack capability—they lack bandwidth ⛓️
Too much time in transactions, too little in decision support.
Here’s a practical roadmap to change that 👇
🔍 Diagnose (Weeks 1–3)
Map P2P, O2C, R2R, Tax
Identify bottlenecks & effort split (often 80:20) 📊
⚙️ Automate (Weeks 4–12)
OCR, RPA, reconciliations, GST engines
👉 Focus on high-volume tasks first
📉 50–70% reduction in manual effort
🧱 Build Data Foundation (Weeks 8–16)
Clean master data + integrate systems 🔗
📈 Enable real-time, reliable reporting
💡 Shift to Insights (Weeks 12–20)
Dashboards, forecasting, variance analysis 📊
🤝 Finance becomes a decision partner
🧠 Redefine Team (Weeks 16–24)
Reskill + new KPIs (impact > timelines)
👥 Move from processors to advisors
🔄 Continuous Optimization
AI-led forecasting, benchmarking, automation 🚀
⚡ Quick start:
1️⃣ Automate one process
2️⃣ Build one dashboard
3️⃣ Free 20% capacity → move to analysis
💭 Finance transformation isn’t about doing things faster.
It’s about doing the right things.
