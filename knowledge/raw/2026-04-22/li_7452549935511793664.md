## 원문

🔥Most AI conversations are focused on tools.🔥
The real shift is much bigger than that.
After multiple strategy sessions last week, one thing is very clear:
AI is not the disruption.
It’s the catalyst for rethinking how Customer Experience actually operates.
For years, CX scaled on one equation:
Offshore = Cost Optimization
That model is now evolving.
Leaders are asking a more important question:
→ What should be handled by AI, and where do humans create the most value?
🚨🚨Not 5 years from now, but Right now!!
Here’s what’s changing fast:
• AI is already automating 30–50% of Tier 1 interactions in leading CX organizations
• Cost per contact is improving by 25–40% with AI-led workflows
• Yet 80% of AI initiatives still don’t scale beyond pilots
This isn’t a technology gap.
It’s an opportunity to lead differently.
The organizations making real progress are getting clear on three things:
• Where AI drives efficiency
• Where humans elevate the experience
• How success is measured beyond activity
This is where AI Adoption and Operating Models become the differentiator.
Not more tools, but better decisions.
The next phase of Customer Experience will be defined by how intentionally we redesign work across AI and human collaboration.
That’s where the real value is.
