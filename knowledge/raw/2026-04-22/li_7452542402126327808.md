## 원문

From Copilots
To Agents
Why Agentic AI is not what most enterprises think.
Everyone is talking about Agentic AI.
But in most enterprise environments, it is still being treated like an extension of copilots. That is the wrong model. What I am seeing across government, financial services, and regulated industries is a different shift emerging.
Not AI as a feature.
Not AI as a tool.
AI as an operational layer.
Agentic AI changes how systems behave:
- Platforms provide the foundation
- Agents orchestrate and adapt
- Humans remain accountable for outcomes
This is where the real transformation sits. Because the value is not in autonomy alone. It is in augmentation.
The highest performing teams are not removing humans from the loop. They are elevating them:
- better decisions
- faster execution
- clearer focus on business outcomes
And in regulated environments, this is not optional.
You cannot experiment your way into production AI.
You need:
- strong architecture
- clear governance
- secure by design platforms
The industry is still heavily focused on models and copilots. But the real shift is happening at the system level.
From tools --> To systems
From assistance --> To orchestration
Over the coming weeks, I will break down what this actually looks like in enterprise. Particularly across AWS and hybrid cloud environments.
If you are working through this shift, I would value your perspective.
