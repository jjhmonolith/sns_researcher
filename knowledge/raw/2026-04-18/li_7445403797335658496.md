Coming from a country that has strong AI governance can actually be an advantage and a booster.
South Korea having an AI Law has enabled Justin Eom to navigate how AI should be implemented in schools.
Whilst the UAE nor the UK have that, they do both have frameworks.
However, when talking to Justin in our conversation the UAE's truly diverse education ecosystem makes it the perfect launch pad for AI, and especially AI education businesses, as I discuss in the podcast.
In the UAE, you can find French curriculum schools, American, British, Indian, Filipino, and so much more. Justin discusses how this is a massive advantage in terms of experimentation and launching into different markets. Justin Eom is currently providing support for schools in the UAE support both students and teachers. do please contact him and Codle.
Watch the full episode here: https://lnkd.in/e_iuqAG9
