Introducing ATLAS — Best International Innovator Award 2024.
A – Autonomous | T – Tracked | L – Litter | A – Assortment | S – Solar-powered
ATLAS is a fully autonomous AI rover, engineered entirely from scratch at the Innovation Hub.
The vision system is built on OpenMV AI computer vision, running real-time object detection, classification, and autonomous decision-making onboard, without any external processing or human input.
The machine sees an object. Identifies it. Decides where it belongs. Picks it up. Places it. Independently.
Every component was designed and fabricated from raw materials. The chassis and body structure, the rubber tracks, the multi-DOF robotic arm, all built from zero, no off-the-shelf parts, no kits. Solar-powered for full field autonomy.
No shortcuts. This is what it means to build from the ground up.
